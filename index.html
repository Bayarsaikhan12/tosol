ABOUT US

The opening caremony of the Baku Tennis Academy took place on May 5, 2009. President llham Aliyev and his spouse Mehriban Aliyeva attended the caremony.For the first time, a Tennis Academy is being built in the Azerbaijani capital. The construction of the academy, which began in March 2008 in the 8th district of Baku,is 2.7 hectares. The 4070-sqoare-meter are of the complex has all the conditions for this sports. A large-capacity reservoir, pumping station, electrical control room and transformer substation with equipment imported from italy have been built here. These facilities will ensure a reliable supply of water and electricity to the academy. The head of state inspected the indoor training courts and got acquainted with the conditions created here.Here,young people will learn the secrets of tennis width the help of professionals.
